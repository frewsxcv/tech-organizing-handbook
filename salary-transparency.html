Salary transparency

This is a protected concerted activity.

Managers are not protected.

Allow people to retract their submissions.

Permissions

Only employees should be able to see the form and results. If your employer uses G Suite, consider having an organizer host a Google Form in their employer’s Google Drive.

Sample question/form set

  1. Name [Open text]
  2. What is your current title? [Open text]
  3. What is your current salary in USD? [Open text]
  4. Did you negotiate your current salary? [ Y/N ]
    • [if yes] What were you originally offered? [Open text]
  5. When did you last receive a raise? [Multiple choice]
    • July 2019 review cycle
    • January 2019 review cycle
    • I received a raise outside of the review cycle
    • I have never received a raise
    • Other
  6. When did you first start at Kickstarter? [Date field]
  7. What was your title when you joined Kickstarter? [Open text]
  8. What was your salary when you started in USD? [Open text]
  9. Did you negotiate your starting salary? [ Y/N ]
    • [If yes] What were you originally offered? [Open text]
  10. How many years of work experience did you have in a same or similar position before joining Kickstarter? [Open text]
  11. If you receive or have received additional compensation outside of salary and company-wide benefits, please elaborate here. [Open text]
